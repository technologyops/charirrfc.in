Brief Biography                                                      Photography



  • Born on 18th August 1944 at Cuddlaore, TamilNadu, India.

  • Educated at Bombay and U.K.

  • Professional Engineer.

  • Worked with Vickers Ltd and IBM in UK.

  • Then worked in IBM, Crompton Greever and Tatas in India before setting up own business in Chennai, TamilNadu.

  • Represents “The Hindu” as a major distributor since 1983.

  • Agencies for many paper products.



SERVICE ORGANISATIONS
  • Active in Committees for Nuclear disarmament in UK as a student.

  • Active member of Rotary.

  • Served in Rotary District Governor.

  • Served as the Editor of Rotary News for 7 years; The news magazine for Rotarians in India with a circulation of 70,000 + copies.

  • Active office bearer in The Rotary Foundation with responsibilities for fund raising in South Asia.

  • Active in Social mobilization for development causes.
