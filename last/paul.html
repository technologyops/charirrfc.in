THE PAUL HARRIS SOCIETY IS A (DISTRICT-ADMINISTERED AND RECOGNIZED BY THE ROTARY FOUNDATION) NEW LEVEL OF RECOGNITION FOR THOSE WHO ELECT TO GIVE US$1 ,000 OR MORE EACH YEAR TO THE ROTARY FOUNDATION'S ANNUAL PROGRAMS FUND (APF). THROUGH CONTRIBUTIONS TO APF. 50% OF THESE FUNDS ARE RETURNED TO THE DISTRICT IN THREE YEARS AS DISTRICT DESIGNATED FUNDS.

SUGGESTED GUIDELINES
  1. Since contributions to The Permanent Fund do not qualify towards Paul Harris Fellow Recognition, contributions to the Permanent Fund will not qualify for membership in the PHS.

  2. Only new, outright contributions count towards membership. The transfer of Foundation Recognition Points do not count.

  3. Each donor must give a minimum of $1 ,000 annually to qualify. A member and spouse must give a minimum of $1 ,000 each for both to qualify as members of the PHS.

  4. Contributions to club foundations do not qualify towards membership in the PHS.

  5. Membership in the PHS will be determined once your contribution posts at The Rotary Foundation and you alert the person in charge in your district that the payment has posted. District Governor / District Rotary Foundation Chair will designate the person in charge of the The Paul Harris Society register.

  6. Pledges do not count towards membership in the PHS.

  7. District Governor is urged to hold a special recognition event to honor the Paul Harris Society Members every year.

A Sample Solicitation letter

LETTERHEAD

Date

Dear Rtn Krishnan V Chari

Rotarians worldwide share the common goal of making our world a better place. As a dedicated Rotarian, you certainly share the hope of achieving world peace and understanding through the projects and programs of The Rotary Foundation. Our Foundation's ability to serve humanity is limitless as long as we participate in both hands-on projects and provide financial support.

District Governor J B Kamdar, District Rotary Foundation Chairman V.K.Chandra Kumar and I cordially extend an invitation for you to become a Charter Member of The . Paul Harris Society of Rotary International District 3230

Beginning this Rotary year, every donor who annually contributes $1,000 to The Rotary Foundation will be awarded membership in the Paul Harris Society. Society members will receive a special Certificate of Recognition and a distinctive gold and blue lapel banner, which can be worn with their Rotary pin.

District 3230 is fortunate to have dedicated Rotarians with the capacity and desire to become Paul Harris Society members. Please share this information with your club members and join us in the formation of this exciting new district level program that will build a stronger Rotary Foundation.

A PHS Membership Form is enclosed.

Thank you in advance for your consideration and your continued support of the programs of the Rotary Foundation,

You Make the Difference!

Yours in Rotary service,

District 3230 Annual Giving Subcommittee Chair,
