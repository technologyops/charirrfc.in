Brief Biography                                                                         Photography

Chari studied engineering in Mumbai and in the U. K. He has worked in senior management positions for IBM, Crompton, and Tatas. In 1983 he settled down in Chennai, and is currently a distributor for The Hindu, one of India’s national newspapers. He is also associated with businesses in Mumbai and Delhi dealing in paper products and gifts.

A Rotarian since 1983, Chari is a member of the Rotary Club of Meenambakkam, where he served as club president in 1987-88. her served as editor for the Rotary News, India’s regional Rotary magazine, from 1988 to 2005. Chari was District Trainer in 1999, represented the President at the District 3190 Conference in 2002, and served as Chief District counselor in 2001 – 02.

Recent Committees:
  • 2005 Chicago Convention Promotion (Member, 2004 – 05)

  • Presidential Celebration Committee Literacy (Member, 2004 – 05)

  • District Grants Subcommittee (Chairperson, 2001-02)

  • District Annual Giving Subcommittee (Chairperson, 2001-02)


While serving district 3230 as “Caring Governor” in 1997-98, Chari oversaw many projects that improved the lives of people in his district, including a pre-primary education program; a “meal day” program to feed orphans; and a functional literacy and vocational skills program, among others. He raised over US $120,000 for the Foundation while Governor, and oversaw the implementation of many Matching Grants by his district.
